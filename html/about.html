About Us

About findME

findME is a location-based event discovery platform that helps you explore activities happening near you — from concerts and workshops to festivals and wellness events. Whether you're new in town or just want something fun to do, findME connects you to your local community.

Our Mission

We created findME to make it easier for people to discover exciting, relevant, and nearby events. Our goal is to encourage real-world experiences and community engagement through technology.

How It Works

findME uses your location to suggest events that match your interests and are happening close by. You can browse categories like sport, entertainment, educational, and more — all organized for easy exploration.
